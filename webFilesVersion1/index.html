Story

Problem

  1. Many people are hungry (842 million worlwide, 1 in 6 Americans go hungry (49 million Americans do not have dependable, consistent access to food; 807,000 are food insecure in Chicago)
  2. Food pantries are understaffed and using 20th century technology
  3. YET, there is still a large amount of food that is not redistributed
There are apps out there that to help people locate the nearest food pantry, but there's no application that gives you a donate FOOD now button. There ought to be a way for people to know not only where to give, but HOW. What's lacking are convenience and immediacy.
Conversely, pantries, soup kitchens, and shelters all have different needs, drop off times, storage capacity, and pick-up capacity. Fed Up(load) seeks to help these organizations streamline the food donation intake process, cutting multiple phone calls and emails down to one simple means of communication.
